Mitsubishi Heavy Industries - my failed success

December 26, 2024

The pandemic was a seismic event for the entire market. Stocks stayed cheap for quite some time - some even lingered at bargain-bin prices longer than others. Industrials, for example. And what fascinates me - at least personally - is how people manage to become absurdly pessimistic precisely when things hit rock bottom. Take Mitsubishi Heavy Industries (MHI) trading at 2500 or so in 2021. People were practically eulogizing the company, saying, “This company is dead, and here’s a 1000-tweet thread explaining why (1/1000).” I’m not exaggerating, but if these kinds of idiots were told to eat shit, humanity would instantly lose its classification as omnivores.
